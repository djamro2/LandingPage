Welcome Week 2016

Welcome week is a chance to experience that the city has to offer with a series of 8 unforgettable events at NYC’s hottest nightclubs. comes up, we are the experts. All parties are 18+ or College I.D. to enter. We release the lineup in August but expect to party with 10,000 of your classmates at everything from crazy raves, to elegant balls. So get ready to party till the sun comes up! Feel free to e-mail (mailto link info@nycwelcomeweek.com) us with any questions and make sure to follow us on Inastagram & Snapchat. Don’t forget to check out the pictures and videos from last year’s events to get an idea of what’s in store!

What is it?

A week of non stop crazy events from Yacht Parties to Raves to Dress to Impress Gatsby Parties in Manhattan’s hottest nightclubs. Offering 18+ access to the best of NYC Nightlife for one week only

Whose going?

Over 10,000 of your new classmates from NYU, FIT, LIM, PACE, PRATT, NYIT, SVA, NYU POLY, NEW SCHOOL / PARSONS, COLUMBIA, BARNARD, JUILLIARD, MARYMOUNT MANHATTAN, MANHATTAN COLLEGE, FORDHAM, ST JOHNS, COOPER UNION, HUNTER, & BARUCH!

When is it?

NYC Welcome Week takes place August 28th- September 4th. In addition to the welcome week, there will also be 3 pre-welcome week parties the week prior for the schools whose mov in dates are earlier.
