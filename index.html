Welcome Week 2016

Welcome week is a weeklong party. There is a party every night and no class the next day! This orientation period has been a tradition for new and returning students alike to explore the city by day and party until sunrise by night. This year, we’ve booked an array of beautiful lounges and crazy nightclubs that will all have unique environments, amazing theme nights, and insane music. There will definitely be something for everyone so don’t be stuck playing drinking games in your dorm room – come out, make new friends, and start the year off right!
