Welcome Week 2016

Welcome week is a weeklong party. There is a party every night and no class the next day! This orientation period has been a tradition for new and returning students alike to explore the city by day and party until sunrise by night. This year, we’ve booked an array of beautiful lounges and crazy nightclubs that will all have unique environments, amazing theme nights, and insane music. There will definitely be something for everyone so don’t be stuck playing drinking games in your dorm room – come out, make new friends, and start the year off right!

What is it?

8 Different Events across Manhattan at the best clubs and lounges NYC has to offer

Whose going?

There will be a total of 10,000 Attendees across the 8 events! The major presence will be from New York University, Pace University and The Fashion Institute of Technology!

What are the dates?

The events start on August 26 and will end on September 2nd!

Get Hired

Positions

Promoters

Are you the life of the party? A socially outgoing student who wants to have an amazing time partying AND get paid to bring people to the parties? If you've got the networking and partying skills then this is the job for you! Payment is on a per-head-basis!

Street Team

Are you a friendly and socially outgoing student who would like to distribute promotional materials around college campuses? It's an easy and fun way to meet new people and get paid hourly for it!

Promotional Models

Are you tall and beautiful? Do you have modeling experience? Or have you been told that you could be a model? Now is your chance! Our models help our street team, participate in photo shoots for flyers, and get to party for free whenever they want!! Please include two or three pictures if you are applying for this position.

Photographers

Do you live behind your camera? We need photographers who can take pictures of our insanely crazy welcome week parties and our super hot models!! Please include up to five samples of your photography if you are applying for this position.

Graphic Designers

Can you design flyers for the sickest parties ever? Thousands of these flyers will be distributed around NYC during welcome week! Please create a sample flyer for a party to include if you are applying for this position (please include the following information on the flyer: a celebrity guest/performer , drink specials, date, time, address, etc. - Be creative and impress us!!)
